A.M. Paradox

Christopher Brent Wood, better known by his stage name Brent Faiyaz, is an American singer and record producer. He released his debut project, an extended play entitled A.M. Paradox, in 2016.

Watch Video

Brent Faiyaz, So Far Gone

Faiyaz began uploading his music onto SoundCloud in 2014 and moved from his hometown Columbia, Maryland to Charlotte, Maryland before ultimately settling in Los Angeles, California to further his music career. On January 19, 2015, he released his debut single "Allure". On June 1, 2016, Faiyaz released "Invite Me", the lead single from his upcoming debut EP. On September 19, 2016, his 21st birthday, the EP entitled A.M. Paradox was released and recieved with positive reviews from music critics. On January 26, 2017, Sonder released their dbut EP Intro.
